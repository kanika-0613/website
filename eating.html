Eating Disorder Test (SCOFF)

Answer the following questions with Yes or No.

1. Do you make yourself sick because you feel uncomfortably full?

2. Do you worry you have lost control over how much you eat?

3. Have you recently lost more than one stine(6.35 kg) in a 3-month period?

4. Do you believe yourself to be fat?

5. Would you say that food dominates your life?

6. Do you spend a lot of time planning, counting, or obsessing over calories?
