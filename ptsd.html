PTSD Test (PCL-5 Short Form)

In the past month, how much were you bothered by the following problems?

1. Repeated, disturbing memories of a stressful experience

2. Repeated, disturbing dreams of the experience

3. Suddenly feeling or acting as if the stressful experience were happening again

4. Avoiding thoughts or feelings related to the experience

5. Avoiding places or activities that remind you of the experience

6. trouble sleeping

7. Feeling irritable or having angry outbursts

8. Feeling constantly on guard or watchful
