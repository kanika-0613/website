Bipolar Test (MDQ)

Answer the following questions with Yes or No.

1. Have you had periods of feeling so good or hyper that others thought you were not your normal self?

2. Did you feel much more self-confident than usual?

3. Did you need much less sleep than usual and still feel rested?

4. Were you more talkative or spoke faster thn usual?

5. Did your thoughts race or jump from one topic to another?

6. Were you so easily distracted that you had trouble concentrating?

7. Did you have more energy than usual?

8. Did you you do things that were unusual for oyu ot that could cause trouble?

9. If yes to several above, did all these happen during the same period?
