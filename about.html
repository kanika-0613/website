About Us

Welcome to MindMetrics — a safe and supportive space where you can explore your mental well-being through self-assessments. We provide scientifically recognized screening tools for conditions such as Depression, Anxiety, Bipolar Disorder, PTSD, ADHD, and Eating Disorders.

Our mission is to make mental health resources more accessible. These tests are not a diagnosis, but they can help you reflect on your emotional state and guide you toward seeking professional support when needed.

Remember: reaching out for help is a sign of strength, not weakness. If you ever feel overwhelmed, we encourage you to connect with a licensed mental health professional or call your local support helpline immediately.

You are not alone —your mental health matters.
